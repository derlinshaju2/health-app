✅ Immediate Actions to Try:

  1. Hard Refresh: Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  2. Clear Cache: Press F12 → Right-click refresh button → "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
  3. Check Browser Console: Press F12 → Look for red errors
  4. Try Different Browser: Test in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  5. Check Internet Connection: Ensure you're online and Vercel deployment is live
