Understanding BMI

What is BMI?
Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.
BMI Formula
BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²
Healthy Range
A BMI of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered healthy for most adults.
